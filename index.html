The Misappropriation of Culture in Fantastical Literature; A Result of Rushing to Publication?

Discover how the popularity of online reading communities has led to a surge in published books, but at what expense to cultural authenticity? Dive into the discussion on rushed writing and its impact on respecting diverse cultures, using author Rebecca Yarros's journey as a focal point. Explore the importance of research and respect in fantasy literature, and how authors can navigate these challenges while honoring the cultures they draw inspiration from.
